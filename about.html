My Story and Why This Journey Matters

At 66, and after a significant battle with cancer, embarking on this road trip in my self-converted 1999 Ford Astro van with my two beloved small dogs (a mini schnauzer and a peekaboo) is more than just a vacation. It's a testament to resilience, a pursuit of freedom, and a new chapter in my life. This journey through the Ozarks to Montana is about embracing the present, finding peace on the open road, and creating lasting memories.

Every mile I travel is a step forward in my recovery, reminding me of the strength within. The companionship of my dogs is a constant source of joy and comfort, making every stop and every view even more special.

I believe in living life to the fullest, and this adventure is my way of doing just that. Thank you for taking the time to learn about my journey and for your incredible support.
